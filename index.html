~/about_us.md

$ cat about.md

We're a community of students who believe college should be more than just lectures and attendance sheets. It's a place to experiment, build cool stuff, and learn by breaking things.

Every week, we meet to discuss technology, work on projects, and explore everything from web development to cybersecurity. Think of it as your escape from the monotony: a creative outlet where you can apply classroom theory to real-world problems.

"The best way to learn is to build something you care about, break it repeatedly, and fix it until it works."

We're not just about coding. We're about creating a culture where curiosity is encouraged, where late-night debugging sessions turn into friendships, and where the lab becomes your second home.

What We Do:

  • ▸ Regular tech discussions and rapid-fire learning sessions
  • ▸ Hands-on projects in open source development
  • ▸ Cybersecurity workshops and CTF challenges
  • ▸ Regular excursions and tech events
  • ▸ System administration and infrastructure learning
